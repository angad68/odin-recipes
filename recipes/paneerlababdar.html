Paneer Lababdar

Paneer Lababdar is a delectable North Indian dish known for its rich and creamy tomato-based gravy with succulent pieces of paneer (Indian cottage cheese). This dish is a favorite among vegetarians and non-vegetarians alike, owing to its irresistible flavors and smooth texture. Originating from the kitchens of Punjab, Paneer Lababdar is a celebration of traditional spices and ingredients, offering a burst of flavors with every bite. Whether served with hot naan bread or steamed rice, Paneer Lababdar is sure to tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

  • Paneer (Indian cottage cheese)
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Cashew Nuts
  • Cream
  • Butter
  • Spices (e.g., cumin, coriander, turmeric, garam masala)
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Prepare paneer: Cut paneer into cubes and set aside.
  2. Prepare gravy: In a pan, saut√© onions, ginger, and garlic until golden brown. Add chopped tomatoes and cashew nuts. Cook until tomatoes are soft and pulpy.
  3. Blend: Allow the mixture to cool, then blend it into a smooth paste.
  4. Cook gravy: In the same pan, heat butter and add the blended paste. Cook until the mixture thickens and the raw smell disappears.
  5. Add spices: Add turmeric, cumin, coriander, and garam masala powder. Mix well and cook for a few minutes.
  6. Finish dish: Add cream and stir until well combined. Then, add paneer cubes and simmer for a few minutes until the paneer absorbs the flavors of the gravy.
  7. Serve: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan bread or rice.
