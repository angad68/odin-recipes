Butter Chicken

Butter chicken, a beloved dish in Indian cuisine, tantalizes the palate with its rich and creamy tomato-based sauce infused with a blend of aromatic spices. Tender pieces of marinated chicken are simmered to perfection, absorbing the flavors of the sauce while remaining succulent and flavorful. Originating from the kitchens of Delhi, this iconic dish is a harmonious fusion of tangy tomatoes, velvety butter, and a hint of sweetness, creating a delightful balance of flavors that keeps diners coming back for more. Ingredients

  • Cashew Nuts
  • Raw Chicken (500g)
  • Cream
  • Butter
  • Spices
  • Tomatoes
  • Yogurt

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken: Mix cubed chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, and a blend of spices like garam masala, turmeric, and chili powder. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Cook chicken: Sauté marinated chicken in a pan until it's lightly browned and cooked through. Set it aside.
  3. Prepare sauce: In the same pan, sauté onions, garlic, and ginger until soft. Add tomato puree, cream, and butter. Simmer until the sauce thickens and the flavors meld together.
  4. Combine and serve: Add the cooked chicken to the sauce. Simmer for a few more minutes until everything is heated through and well combined. Serve hot with rice or naan bread.
